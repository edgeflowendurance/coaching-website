Background

Athlete First.
Coach Always.

I am a competitive age group triathlete who has raced everything from local sprints to 140.6s for 15+ years. As a full-time data scientist and having a Ph.D., I coach athletes with a scientific and data-driven mindset. Having worked through the same challenges every age grouper faces — balancing training with a full-time career, managing injuries, dialing in nutrition on long race days — there's an art to finding the balance and it has come through hard-earned experience and a deep toolbox of approaches that actually work.


The coaching philosophy is rooted in evidence: structured training blocks, zone-based intensity distribution, clear focus on recovery, and continuous refinement of your weakest discipline. You'll always know why you're doing a workout, not just what it is. I believe in building the training plan with the athlete, with constant and open feedback from the athlete about their physical and mental well-being.

Training Philosophy

Smart Training
Beats More Training

More hours don't automatically equal more speed. The athletes who improve fastest are the ones doing the right work at the right intensity at the right time. That's what every Edge Flow plan is built around: pyramidal training distribution, discipline-specific focus blocks, and recovery baked in as a non-negotiable.


Race strategy is treated as seriously as training. You'll go into every race with a detailed pacing plan, a nutrition protocol, and a mental framework — because the best fitness in the world won't matter if you blow up at mile 40 on the bike.

FAQ

Common Questions

What platform do you use for training plans?

I use TrainingPeaks for all plan delivery and workout tracking. A free account is all you need to get started.

Do I need a power meter to train with Edge Flow?

A power meter is helpful but not required. I highly recommend one — it's the most reliable way to control and measure your training load.

How far out should I start coaching before my race?

Ideally 16–24 weeks. This gives us time for base building, a focused build block, a peak phase, and a proper taper. Less time is workable — reach out and I'll find the right approach.

Is there a contract or minimum commitment?

No long-term contracts. I ask for a 3-month initial commitment to see real results, and after that it's month-to-month. You can pause or cancel with 30 days notice.
